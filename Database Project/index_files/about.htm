The TechForge Story

Who We Are

TechForge is a student-led initiative based at Rutgers University-Newark. We bridge the gap between complex computer science theory and high-performance hardware reality. Whether you are a fellow student needing a study rig or a professional creator in the city, we build with precision.

Our Build Tiers

Budget: Optimized for daily productivity and reliable office workflows.
Pro: Built for high-frame-rate gaming and smooth content creation.
Ultra: The gold standard. 4K gaming, heavy 3D rendering, and extreme multitasking.
